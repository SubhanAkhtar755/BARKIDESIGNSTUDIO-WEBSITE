Modern Farmhouse Plan - 2,400 Sq. Ft.

2,400 Sq. Ft.
4 Bedrooms
3.5 Bathrooms
2 Car Garage

This stunning Modern Farmhouse combines contemporary design with traditional farmhouse charm. The exterior features board and batten siding, a metal roof accent, and a welcoming front porch. The open-concept main floor includes a spacious great room with vaulted ceilings, a gourmet kitchen with large island, and a dining area that opens to a covered patio. The second floor houses the luxurious master suite with private balcony, three additional bedrooms, and a convenient laundry room.
