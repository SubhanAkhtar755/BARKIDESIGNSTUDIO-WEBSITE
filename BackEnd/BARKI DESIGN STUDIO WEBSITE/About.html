Our Story

About Barki Design Studio LLC Since 2019, Barki Design Studio LLC has been providing professional custom design services for Home & Commercial Builders as well as for Home Clients. We will work with you to design the house of your dreams, within your budget and time frame. We are committed to meeting your needs with professional precision. As a licensed engineering firm, we provide a full range of computer-aided drafting, design, and engineering services for clients across Texas. Our team ensures that every project complies with the applicable building codes and local requirements, while delivering accurate, code-compliant plans tailored to your project. You'll appreciate our fast turnaround, our experience, and our professionalism. We know that deadlines can be tight and that urgent assignments can develop with little or no warning. For builders, architects and engineers, Barki Design Studio LLC can enhance your company's staff by providing reliable, confidential help for your projects.
